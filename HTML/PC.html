  • BEST OF PS5

    • 1. VALORANT

    Riot Games released VALORANT last year after keeping the game in closed beta for a long time. The 5v5 multiplayer tactical shooter offers incredibly competitive gameplay. And that’s why in just over a year, Riot’s brainchild has become one of the biggest esports titles. Several CS:GO professional players switched to VALORANT because of its modern gameplay. So, if you haven’t tried out the FPS title yourself, maybe you should give it a shot in 2022. Also, you don’t have to think twice before downloading the game, considering that it’s completely free.

    2. LEAGUE OF LEGENDS

    Here we are with another Riot Games title for PC that you could play for free. If you don’t know already, League of Legends is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game with more than 115 million players from around the world. Besides that, the multiplayer title is a big sensation in the esports community.If you love multiplayer games that force you to strategize to outsmart your opponent, League of Legends is definitely worth trying. Of course, with multiple legends, the game might feel overwhelming to you. But once you get the hang of it, you won’t be playing any other game for a long time.

    3. LOST ARK

    The story begins in Arkesia, a fictional world that, after having 500 years of peace thanks to seven artifacts called 'arks', is invaded by a powerful horde of demons.As a hero of the adventure, your mission will be to travel each continent to gather them and bravely face the enemies.Of course, the road will not be easy, since each region has its own local threats that will sometimes force you to postpone your main objective to lend a hand.

    4. MULTIPLATYER HALO INFINITE

    Every Halo fan was eager to play the latest installment of the franchise in 2021. After a long wait, 343 Industries finally released Halo Infinite on December 8th, 2021, and honestly, the game didn’t disappoint.Of course, Halo Infinite features one of the best campaigns in the franchise. However, for several players, the major highlight is the Multiplayer Mode, which, of course, is available to play for everyone for free.

    5. SCAVENGERS

    Developed by Midwinter Entertainment, Scavengers is a free-to-play action shooter. You can play as a squad of three in the game and try out unique weapons and abilities. The multiplayer titles offer both PvE and PvP experience, so you have to strategize around different things to survive in the harsh world of Scavengers; the best part, you can do all that without paying a single penny.
